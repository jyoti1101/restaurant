ABOUT US

OUR HISTORY

In order to meet the aspiration of students of AGRA to get quality computer education, Shri Raj Computer & Welfare Society (N.G.O) took the initiative in this field, and the Shri Raj Computer Educational Institute (SRCEI) was established in May 2004 as a part of welfare activity. Initially it was started with very limited facilities and with a few students. Now it has become a prestigious training institute of this area having the best infrastructure among computer institutes in non-formal sector in this region and has been providing best possible facilities to the students helping in gaining in depth knowledge of the relevant subjects.
